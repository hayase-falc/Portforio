UE5 Portforio

Created Open World RPG Tutrial on Unreal Engine5 with Blueprint only.

I'm Hayase Ogawa. I'm under development of Open World RPG.
I learned Unreal Enigne for about three years and I create 3d model for four years.

  • Most of animation are controlled in Animation Blueprint.
  • Behavier Tree was defined as enemy control.
  • Most of component are defined as Actor Component for memory load to light.
  • Items are composed of Structure, and it can be used on recieved achieve of Quest or Tresure Chest.
  • Most of animation was imported from Mixamo, and I adjust them to fit on my game.
  • Icons in my game are imported from FLATICON and FreeIcon, etc, so it will not be able sale unless I refer to them in game, or private for use.
  • This game is under development. I'll add contents of Story, Gimmics and Skills in terms of Sysytem Dev.
  • Finally, I'll create design of UI, Levels, Character and Items.
